Home

TSM (Team SoloMid) is one of North America's oldest and most well-known eSports brands. Since their creation in 2011, TSM has qualified for every Worlds Championship, and has never failed to place top two in North America. However, North America has always been regarded internationally as one of the weaker regions in League of Legends, never making it far on the world stage. This year's TSM roster is looking like the best team North America has ever produced, coming off of an absolutely dominating 17-1 record during the regular season. This could be North America's biggest chance to finally bring home the World Cup.
